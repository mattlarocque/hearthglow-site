Privacy Notice

Effective date: June 23, 2026 · Last updated: June 23, 2026 · Contact: privacy@hearthglow.ca

1. About This Notice

Hearthglow is committed to protecting your privacy. This notice explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and how long we keep it. It applies to all customers who request a quote, accept a service agreement, or access our customer portal.

We are regulated by the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

2. What Personal Information We Collect

When you request a quote or use our services, we collect:

  • Contact information: Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Property information: The address of the property where we'll install lights
  • Service preferences: Your preferred service tier, install window, and any special notes or requests
  • Photos: Images of your property (for measuring and designing your installation)
  • Quote and agreement: Your accepted quote, agreed scope of work, and installation dates
  • Payment information: Invoice amounts, deposit status, and final balance (we do not store your full credit card number — our payment processor handles that)
  • Consent records: Whether you've agreed to receive marketing emails from us
  • Technical information: Your IP address and the date/time when you accepted your quote (for security and to prove when you consented)

3. Why We Collect This Information

We collect your information to:

  • Create and deliver your custom quote
  • Confirm your agreement to our service terms
  • Process your deposit and final payment
  • Schedule and deliver your seasonal installation and teardown
  • Communicate with you about your quote, invoice, and service (transactional emails)
  • Comply with tax and legal obligations (Canada Revenue Agency record-keeping)
  • Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Comply with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) by maintaining proof of your consent to marketing communications

4. How We Use Your Information

Transactional Communications

We will always send you emails about:

  • Your quote and agreement
  • Your invoice and payment status
  • Your scheduled installation and teardown dates
  • Any updates or issues related to your service

You cannot opt out of transactional emails — they're essential to deliver your service.

Marketing Communications

We only send marketing emails (like seasonal offers or next year's installation reminders) if you've explicitly agreed to receive them. In December, we'll ask: "May we contact you in the future about next year's installation or seasonal offers?" If you agree, we'll add you to our marketing list. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email.

5. Who We Share Your Information With

We share your personal information only with trusted service providers and contractors who help us deliver our service:

Service Providers

  • Stripe (payment processor): We share your name, email, and quote/deposit amounts so Stripe can process your payment. Stripe does not see your full credit card number. (Stripe's privacy policy)
  • Amazon Web Services (S3) (data storage): We store your photos and documents in Canada (ca-central-1). (AWS's privacy policy)
  • CanSpace (future web hosting): As we grow, we plan to migrate our website and database to CanSpace, a Canadian hosting provider.

Subcontractors (Installation Crews)

Hearthglow engages independent installation crews to perform service delivery. When your job is assigned to an installation crew, we share with that crew the information necessary to complete your service: your first name, service address, job details, any notes you provided during the quoting process, and photos you submitted as part of your quote.

This disclosure is limited to the assigned crew for your specific job. Installation crews are not authorized to retain or use your information for any other purpose. Hearthglow remains responsible for the privacy obligations in this notice.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any other third parties.

6. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Photos, quotes, invoices, and most personal data: At least 6 years. We archive photos at the end of each season for our records and to comply with Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) tax audit requirements.
  • CASL consent records: 6 years (CASL requires minimum 3 years; we retain longer for audit robustness).
  • IP address and timestamp: 6 years (retained as proof of when you consented to our terms).

After 6 years, we delete your information. In rare cases, we may retain certain data longer if required by law (e.g., a customer dispute under investigation). We do not retain data indefinitely for operational reasons.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

Access Your Information

You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you. Email us at privacy@hearthglow.ca with your name and email address. We'll send you a complete record within 30 days.

Correct Your Information

You can update your phone number and address directly in your customer portal (changes take effect immediately), or email privacy@hearthglow.ca for other corrections. We'll confirm receipt within 2 business days.

Withdraw Marketing Consent

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email, or by emailing privacy@hearthglow.ca. We'll update your record within 2 business days.

Delete Your Information (With Limits)

You can request that we delete your photos, notes, and contact information. We will comply within 30 days. However, we must retain:

  • Financial records (invoices and quotes) for 6 years per tax law
  • CASL consent records for 6 years per our data retention policy

Process for Rights Requests

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@hearthglow.ca with:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • A clear description of what you're requesting (access, correction, deletion, or withdraw consent)

We will respond within 30 days. If we cannot fulfill your request, we'll explain why.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypting data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Limiting access to your information to authorized staff only
  • Storing your data with trusted Canadian and North American service providers
  • Regular security reviews and updates

However, no security system is 100% secure. If you have security concerns, please contact us at privacy@hearthglow.ca.

9. Consent and Changes to This Notice

By submitting a quote request or accepting our service agreement, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this notice.

We may update this notice from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll post any changes here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after changes means you accept the updated notice.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this notice, your privacy, or how we handle your information:

Email: privacy@hearthglow.ca

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you can also file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca.

11. Definitions

PIPEDA: The Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, Canada's federal privacy law for private-sector organizations.

CASL: Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation. It requires us to get your consent before sending marketing emails, and to maintain proof of that consent.

Personal Information: Any information that identifies you or can be linked to you (name, email, phone, address, photos, etc.).

Transactional Email: Communication essential to delivering your service (quote, invoice, installation dates, service updates).

Marketing Email: Communication about our seasonal offers, next year's services, or other promotional content.
